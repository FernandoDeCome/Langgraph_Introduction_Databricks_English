# TechMart Policies — Online Electronics Store

## Return and Exchange Policy

### Right of Withdrawal
- Customers may cancel their purchase within **7 calendar days** of receiving the product, in accordance with consumer protection law.
- The product must be in its original packaging, with no signs of use.
- Refunds will be issued via the same payment method used for the purchase, within 10 business days.

### Exchange for Defective Products
- Products with manufacturing defects can be exchanged within **30 days** of purchase.
- For defective products after 30 days, the customer must contact the manufacturer's warranty service.
- TechMart offers **free pickup** for defect exchanges nationwide.

### Products Not Eligible for Exchange
- Software with an already activated license.
- Custom-made or special-order products.
- Personal hygiene items (opened in-ear headphones).

## Shipping Policy

### Free Shipping
- Orders over **$299.00** qualify for free shipping nationwide.
- Specific promotions may offer free shipping with no minimum purchase.

### Delivery Timeframes
- **Major metro areas (East Coast)**: 2 to 4 business days.
- **Major metro areas (other regions)**: 4 to 7 business days.
- **Rural areas**: 7 to 12 business days.
- **Express delivery (metro areas)**: 1 to 2 business days (additional fee of $29.90).

### Tracking
- All orders include a tracking number sent via email.
- Tracking becomes available within 24 hours of shipment.

## Payment Methods

### Wire Transfer / ACH
- **5% discount** on payments via wire transfer or ACH.
- Payment confirmed instantly.
- Payment link valid for 30 minutes.

### Credit Card
- Installment plans up to **12 interest-free payments** for purchases over $200.00.
- Minimum installment of $50.00.
- Accepted brands: Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express.

### Bank Transfer
- **3% discount** on payments via bank transfer.
- Payment due within 3 business days.
- Order confirmed after payment clears (1 to 2 business days).

## Warranty

### Legal Warranty
- All products carry a **90-day legal warranty**, as required by consumer protection law.

### Manufacturer's Warranty
- Most products include a **12-month manufacturer's warranty**.
- Notebooks and smartphones: 12-month manufacturer's warranty.
- Accessories: 3 to 6 months depending on the manufacturer.

### TechMart Extended Warranty
- Available for purchase at the time of order.
- Options: **+12 months** (8% of product value) or **+24 months** (14% of product value).
- Covers manufacturing defects beyond the original warranty period.
- **Does not cover** damage from misuse, drops, or liquid contact.

## TechPoints Loyalty Program

### How It Works
- For every **$1.00 spent**, the customer earns **1 TechPoint**.
- Points are valid for **12 months** from the date earned.

### Redeeming Points
- **500 points** → $25.00 coupon
- **1,000 points** → $60.00 coupon
- **2,000 points** → $150.00 coupon
- **5,000 points** → $400.00 coupon

### Program Tiers
- **Bronze** (0 to 999 points): basic benefits.
- **Silver** (1,000 to 4,999 points): free shipping with no minimum + 10% bonus points.
- **Gold** (5,000+ points): free shipping + priority support + 20% bonus points + exclusive offers.
