### What is the difference between SSD and HDD?
- **SSD** (Solid State Drive): much faster, silent, impact-resistant. Recommended for all uses.
- **HDD** (Hard Disk Drive): slower, but offers more capacity at a lower price. Good as secondary storage.
- All TechMart notebooks come with factory-installed SSDs.
